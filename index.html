Why Madagascar?

The Red Island

Madagascar, also nicknamed the eighth continent, is undoubtedly one of the world's leading tourist destinations. Its cultural richness, its endemic fauna and flora make its reputation as a paradise island. This multi-faceted destination has something for everyone: high mountains, picturesque plateaus, numerous natural parks, exotic forests, magnificent beaches…

Still attached to its history and age-old traditions, notably the “famadihana” and the cult of the ancestors, the Malagasy population has other cultural identities to discover during a trip.
